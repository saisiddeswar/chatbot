# RVRJCCE Campus Facilities and Student Services

## Campus Infrastructure

### Academic Buildings
- Modern classrooms equipped with multimedia facilities
- Lecture halls with projectors and internet connectivity

### Laboratories and Practice Areas
- **Electronics Labs**: Equipped with oscilloscopes, power supplies, and measurement instruments

- **Manufacturing Lab**: CNC machines, 3D printers, fabrication equipment

### Academic Services

**Student Portal Access:**
- Login for grades and transcripts
- Attendance records viewable

- Academic calendar and schedules
- Assignment submission and feedback

**Examination Support:**
- Exam cell handles registration and schedule
- Seating arrangements and invigilation
- Result processing and publication
- Grievance handling for exam issues
- Contact: 9985271179, 9966641530

**Certificate Services:**
- Bonafide Certificates: For admission to higher education, employment verification
- Transcripts: Official academic records with marks and grades
- Original Degree (OD): Final degree certification
- Conduct Certificates: Character certificates for placements
- Provisional Certificates: Temporary documents during processing
- Processing time: 3-5 working days after application

**Service Requirements:**
- Application form submission (offline or online)
- Valid student ID required
- No pending dues on student account
- Specific fee for each certificate type
- Multiple copies available if needed
