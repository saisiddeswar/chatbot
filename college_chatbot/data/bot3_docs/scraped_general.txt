
RVR & JC College of Engineering (Rayapati Venkata Ranga Rao & Jagarlamudi Chandramouli College of Engineering) is a private, autonomous institution located in Chowdavaram, Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India.
Established in 1985, it is approved by the AICTE and affiliated with Acharya Nagarjuna University, Guntur.

Location: Chowdavaram, Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India.
Type: Private, Autonomous
Affiliation: Acharya Nagarjuna University

It offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs in various engineering and management disciplines.
The college is known for its strong academic record and infrastructure.
