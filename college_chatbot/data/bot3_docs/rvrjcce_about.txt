# R.V.R. & J.C. College of Engineering - About the Institution

## Admission Process

### Undergraduate Admissions
Admissions to B.Tech programs are conducted as per Government of Andhra Pradesh regulations:

1. **EAPCET Counselling (70% seats)**: Based on rank in Engineering Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test
2. **Management Quota (30% seats)**: Merit/rank-based selections as per government directions
3. **Lateral Entry (10% seats in 2nd year)**: For Diploma holders via ECET

### Eligibility Criteria:
- Minimum qualification: 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry (MPC)
- EAPCET rank required for 70% seats
- Statutory reservations as per government norms

All admissions are ratified by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

### Postgraduate Admissions
- **M.C.A.**: Based on entrance examination and merit
- **M.B.A.**: Based on entrance examination and merit
- **M.Tech.**: Based on GATE score and merit
- Contact Admissions for specific eligibility and application procedures
