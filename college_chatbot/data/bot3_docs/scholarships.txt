Academic Scholarships:

The college provides financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections to support their education. Academic scholarships are awarded to students whose family income is less than Rs. 1,00,000 per year.

During the academic year 2006–2007, scholarships were awarded to 74 students, with a total amount of Rs. 7,40,000 disbursed.

In the academic year 2007–2008, 126 students received scholarships amounting to Rs. 12,60,000.

During 2008–2009, scholarships were provided to 149 students, with a total disbursement of Rs. 16,40,000.

In the academic year 2011–2012, 44 students benefited from scholarships totaling Rs. 6,60,000.

For the academic year 2012–2013, scholarships were awarded to 105 students, with a total amount of Rs. 18,55,000.

In 2013–2014, 155 students received scholarships amounting to Rs. 29,30,000.

During the academic year 2014–2015, 153 students were supported through scholarships totaling Rs. 28,80,000.

In the academic year 2015–2016, scholarships were awarded to 227 students, with a total amount of Rs. 32,90,000.

During 2016–2017, 239 students received scholarships amounting to Rs. 38,00,000.

In the academic year 2017–2018, 260 students benefited from scholarships totaling Rs. 42,60,000.

For the academic year 2018–2019, scholarships were provided to 288 students, with a total disbursement of Rs. 46,06,000.

During 2019–2020, 297 students received scholarships amounting to Rs. 47,02,000.

In the academic year 2023–2024, scholarships were awarded to 277 students, with a total amount of Rs. 55,40,000.

Overall, a total of 2,394 students have benefited from academic scholarships, and the cumulative scholarship amount disbursed by the college is Rs. 3,81,63,000.
