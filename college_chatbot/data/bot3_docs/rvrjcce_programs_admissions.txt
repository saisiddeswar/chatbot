# RVRJCCE Programs and Admissions

## Undergraduate B.Tech Programs

### Overview
RVRJCCE offers 10 diverse B.Tech programs catering to different engineering disciplines and specializations. The undergraduate program is designed with a balanced curriculum covering theoretical foundations and practical applications.

### Core Engineering Programs

**1. Civil Engineering (120 seats)**
- Focus on infrastructure, construction, and structural design
- Curriculum covers surveying, building design, water resources, and transportation
- Practical training through internships and site visits
- Career opportunities in government organizations, construction companies, and consulting firms

**2. Mechanical Engineering (120 seats)**
- Comprehensive training in mechanical systems design and analysis
- Areas include thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, machine design, manufacturing
- Laboratory experience with CAD software and manufacturing equipment
- Placements in automotive, power, and manufacturing sectors

**3. Electronics & Communication Engineering - ECE (180 seats)**
- Training in electronic devices, communication systems, and signal processing
- Specializations in analog/digital electronics, embedded systems, RF communications
- Hands-on experience with electronics labs and communication equipment
- Industry demand from telecom, defense, and semiconductor companies

**4. Electrical & Electronics Engineering - EEE (120 seats)**
- Focus on power systems, electrical machines, and electronics
- Coverage of power generation, transmission, and distribution
- Training in electrical equipment and power electronics
- Career paths in power utilities, industries, and renewable energy sectors

**5. Chemical Engineering (60 seats)**
- Process design, chemical reactions, and industrial operations
- Specializations in petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food processing
- Laboratory work with chemical processing equipment
- Opportunities in chemical, pharmaceutical, and process industries

### Computer Science & Engineering Programs

**6. Computer Science & Engineering - CSE (360 seats)**
- Core CSE program covering programming, databases, algorithms, and software engineering
- Foundation for all computer science specializations
- Strong industry-oriented curriculum with latest programming languages and tools
- Highest enrollment with 360 seats

**7. Information Technology - IT (180 seats)**
- Practical IT focus with emphasis on application development and IT services
- Coverage of web technologies, databases, networking, and IT infrastructure
- Oriented towards IT service companies and software development
- Strong track record in IT industry placements

**8. Computer Science & Business Systems - CSBS (60 seats)**
- Unique integration of computer science and business management
- Curriculum includes both technical CS concepts and business fundamentals
- Prepares students for management and IT consultancy roles
- Ideal for those interested in IT management and business operations

### Specialized CSE Tracks (New Age Programs)

**9. CSE with Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (180 seats)**
- Advanced specialization in AI and ML technologies
- Curriculum covers machine learning algorithms, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision
- Industry-focused projects and emerging technology training
- High demand specialization with excellent placement prospects

**10. CSE with Data Science (180 seats)**
- Specialization focusing on data analytics, big data, and business intelligence
- Training in data processing, statistical analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling
- Hands-on experience with tools like Python, R, SQL, and big data platforms
- Growing field with strong industry demand from tech companies and analytics firms

**11. CSE with Internet of Things - IoT (60 seats)**
- Emerging specialization covering IoT systems, embedded systems, and sensor networks
- Curriculum includes microcontrollers, wireless communication, IoT platforms, and cloud integration
- Practical training with IoT hardware kits and embedded systems
- Career opportunities in smart devices, industrial IoT, and connected systems

### Business Program

**12. Bachelor of Business Administration - B.B.A. (60 seats)**
- Three-year management program for business and entrepreneurship
- Curriculum covers business management, finance, marketing, HR, and operations
- Case studies and business simulations for practical learning
- Prepares for business roles and MBA programs

## Admission Process

### EAPCET (70% of seats)
- Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test conducted by Andhra Pradesh government
- Merit-based seat allotment on EAPCET rank
- Convener manages centralized counselling
- All candidates appear for same entrance test

### Management Quota (30% of seats)
- Reserved for direct admission by the college on merit basis
- Rank-based selection process
- Merit order determined by academic performance
- Subject to government approval and norms

### Lateral Entry - 2nd Year (10% additional seats)
- For Diploma holders in engineering branches
- Engineering Common Entrance Test (ECET) required
- Equivalent to completing 1st year
- Direct admission to 2nd year B.Tech

## Eligibility Criteria

### For Direct B.Tech Admission (1st Year)
- Minimum education: Completed 10+2 with MPC (Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry)
- Subject requirements: Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry mandatory
- Age: No age restrictions
- Nationality: Indian citizens (NRI/OCI candidates eligible)
- EAPCET rank required for 70% seats
- Statutory reservations as per government norms

### For Lateral Entry (2nd Year)
- Minimum education: Completed 3-year Diploma in Engineering
- ECET rank required for seat allotment
- Equivalent status at 2nd year level
- Subject should match with B.Tech branch

## Selection Process

1. **Rank Declaration**: EAPCET/ECET authorities publish merit list and ranks
2. **Counselling**: Centralized counselling by Convener EAPCET conducted
3. **Option Entry**: Candidates exercise choices for college and branch
4. **Seat Allotment**: Seats allocated based on rank and choices
5. **Document Verification**: Candidates verify documents at allotted college
6. **Admission**: Final admission after verification and fee payment
7. **Ratification**: All admissions ratified by Government of AP
