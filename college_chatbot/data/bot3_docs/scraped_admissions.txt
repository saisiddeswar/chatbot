
Admissions to RVR & JC College of Engineering are primarily based on entrance exams.

Undergraduate (B.Tech):
- Admission is based on the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAMCET / AP EAPCET).
- Candidates must have cleared 12th standard with Physics, Maths, and Chemistry as compulsory subjects with a minimum of 45% marks.
- 70% of seats are filled by the Convener, EAPCET (Category-A).
- 30% of seats are filled by the Management (Category-B) based on merit.

Postgraduate (M.Tech):
- Admission is based on GATE or AP PGECET scores.

Management (MBA) and Computer Applications (MCA):
- Admission is based on AP ICET scores.

Key Dates (2026 Cycle):
- AP EAMCET 2026 Registration: Open until March 7, 2026.
- AP EAMCET 2026 Exam: May 12 to 18, 2026.
- GATE 2026 Exam: February 7, 8, 14, and 15, 2026.
